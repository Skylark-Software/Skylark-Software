Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug?

Please email us at support@skylarksoftware.me with details about the issue, including:

  • Device model and iOS/macOS version
  • App version
  • Steps to reproduce the problem
  • Screenshots (if applicable)

How do I request a feature?

We love hearing from our users! Send your feature requests to support@skylarksoftware.me and we'll consider them for future updates.

How do I delete my account and data?

To request account deletion, please email us at support@skylarksoftware.me with your account information. We will process your request within 30 days.
